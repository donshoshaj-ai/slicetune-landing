2. Data We Collect

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Bambu printer model (if provided)
  • Print use (hobby/business/both)
  • Biggest print issue (if provided)
  • Print frequency (if provided)

3. Why We Collect It

We collect this data to run the waitlist and contact you about early access and product updates for SliceTune. We use the information to understand user needs and prioritize development.

4. Processors / Recipients

Form submissions are processed via Formspree (form backend provider). The website is hosted on Netlify.

5. Storage / Retention

We keep your submissions until early access is launched or until you request deletion. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us at slicetune.dev@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority.

7. Cookies & Analytics

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies at this time. Essential technical functions may be used to operate the site (provided by our hosting platform).
